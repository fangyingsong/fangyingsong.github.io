Short Biography

Dr. Fangying Song is a Professor (Qishang Scholar) in College of Mathematics and Computer Science at Fuzhou University. He received the B.S. (2008) and Ph.D. (2014) degrees from Xiamen University in Xiamen, Fujian Province, China. His doctoral advisor is Prof. Chuanju Xu. From Summer 2014 until Summer 2018, he was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Division of Applied Mathematics at Brown University. More information is available on his Google Scholar page.
